Frequently Asked
Who can donate blood?
Anyone aged 18–65, weighing above 50kg, with no major illnesses. Minimum 90 days gap since last donation.
How is a donor selected?
Donors are ranked by blood compatibility, donation history, recency of last donation, and reliability score.
Is my Aadhar data safe?
Yes. Aadhar numbers are used only for identity verification and are never shared with third parties.
How do I update my availability?
Log in as a donor and toggle your availability status in your profile at any time.
